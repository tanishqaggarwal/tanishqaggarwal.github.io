Honors and Awards

Pathfinding Autonomous Navigation (PAN)

A project under the Space Systems Design Studio headed by Dr. Mason Peck, PAN will launch two CubeSats into space on differing orbits and attempt to have them autonomously dock with one another. This project is the first known project to be attempting fully autonomous spacecraft rendezvous and docking manuevers in space. Find out more about the project here.

My job on this team is to engineer the control systems software and hardware that will be responsible for coordinating these manuevers. Using C++, I will be interfacing a Teensy 3.5, our central microcontroller, with the various system peripherals and sensors that will be installed on the spacecraft in order to determine the spacecraft's state.
